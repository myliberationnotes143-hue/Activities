Overview

Corruption refers to the abuse of power for personal gain, often involving bribery, fraud, and other dishonest practices. It exists in both the public and private sectors and can take many forms, from government officials accepting bribes to businesses engaging in illegal practices. The impact of corruption is profound, affecting everything from the economy to the environment, and it perpetuates cycles of inequality and poverty.
